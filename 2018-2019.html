Overview

2018-2019 was a very eventful and exciting year for STEM at Bolsa Grande Highshcool. STEM members participated in many activities, such as the MESA day competition as well as various icebreakers. At the annual Mesa Day competition, 3 teams placed in the regional competition and 1 team made it all the way to the National competition.

Events

Throughout 2018-2019, BGHS's STEM had many events. Here are a list of all (or most) of the events from the year.

Mesa Competition

Preliminary Teams

STEM continued to participate in the annual Mesa Competition where they achieved many accomplishments.

Banquet

STEM's annual banquet at the end of the year was held at Teppanyaki Grill Supreme Buffet on June 1st, 2019 (no photos)

Boba Fundraiser

STEM hosted a Boba fundraiser during this year in order to raise funds for the club's growth and development, as well as for supplies for club members.

Achievements

2017-2018 was a very successful year for BGHS's STEM Club. STEM achieved great achievements during the Mesa Day competition. Some notable achievements are:

3rd Place NEDC State Competition- David Chuong, Minh Duong, Khang Nguyen, and Huy Banh

  • 2nd place 9/10th grade Prosthetic Arm Regional- Bianca Le, Tiffany Tran, Duy Pham
  • 2nd Place MESA Machine Preliminary -Alex Davalos, William Tran, Dererck Bui
  • 2nd place 9/10th Grade Wright Turn Glider Preliminary - Thomas To, Kevin Mach
  • 3rd place Think Tank Preliminary - Hector Flores, Joaquin Lopez, Ryan Dough

Officers

David Chuong

David held the position of President during this year. He was a Senior at the time and had been in STEM for his entire highschool career. He participated in NEDC for 3 years where he made it to regionals, state, and even national competitions.

Hector Flores

Hector held the position of Vice president as a junior during this year. He has been in STEM from the very beginning of his highschool career and hopes to become an aerospace engineer.

Minh Duong
