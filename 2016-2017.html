Overview

2016-2017 was a very exciting year for STEM Club at Bolsa Grande. Many of the STEM members participated in variety of events, such as the MESA Day competition, coding competitions as well as various icebreakers. At the annual MESA Day competition, many Bolsa STEM Teams had placed in top three at Prelims as well as Regionals. Not only that, several outstanding STEM members participated in a hackathon and Congressional App competition, events that challenge students' ability to code and construct products that are applicable in the real world.

Events

2016-2017 year is known for its variety in events and projects. Here is a list of some of the notable events from thatyear:

Congressional App Challenge

This competition is designed to challenge students to create their original, and functional app with the use of many programming languages.

Los Angeles Hackathon

The LA Hackathon competition is an event where computer programmers come together and make a focused effort to perfect a given software product. It involves coding almost continuously for extended periods of time.

MESA National Engineering Design Competition

In this competition, engineers are required to work in a team to implement a Human-Centered Design to address a need. There are many components listed, but the main ones are the successful intergration of Arduino, and a report to provide an overview of the design process
