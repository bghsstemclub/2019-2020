STEM - A Hands-On Experience Club

STEM Club is a gathering of students that meets regularly in a learning environment to work on inquiry-based Science, Technology, Engineering and Math related projects.

STEM strives to offers students to learn more about and get hands-on experience in the STEM fields, be it by constructing engineering projects, coding complex algorithm, or participating in exhilarating and meaningful icebreakers - all with the goal to have them excel in those fields.

Every year, STEM provides students the opportunities to expand their knowledge and compete through a variety of projects, and competitions from different organizations.

Code B

Bolsa Grande's STEM Club is leading the way for students to learn computer sceince with their ongoing program called Code B. The program teaches students step by step how to code and design basic games. Code B gives aspiring computer science majors and enginneers a chance to gain hands on experience with coding.
