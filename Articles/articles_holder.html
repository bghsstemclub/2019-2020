NEWS AND ANNOUNCMENTS

STEM on the Sideline

STEM will be participating in the annual STEM on the Sideline Competition on December 8th. This competition requires students to build a functional device that can launch a football, which solely powered by an arduino board, along with various components in a given sandbox.

Button Fundraiser

STEM Club is having our annual button fundraiser from October 28 to November 1. Our pins start at just 50 cents and come in two sizes: 1 inch and 2.25 inches. We also take custom designs and large club orders. Please tell your friends and family to help support STEM. If anyone is interested, you can contact any officer or advisor.

Code B

Bolsa Grande's STEM Club is leading the way for students to learn computer sceince with their ongoing program called Code B. The program teaches students step by step how to code and design basic games. Code B gives aspiring computer science majors and enginneers a chance to gain hands on experience with coding.

STEM Introductory Meeting

STEM Club will be holding its Introductory Meeting on Saturday, 9/14 at 9:00 AM. This will cover what STEM is about, what projects or competitions it offers and what you can learn from it. Furthermore, we are going to have several ice breakers with some giveaways for the winner!

Mew Tea Fundraiser

Bolsa Grande's STEM Club is back at it with our first fundraiser in the year! We're hosting this at Mew Tea on the Saturday (9/7) from 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM, so please try your best to come out and support your very own STEM club!
